The Most Common Premier League Injuries

Naturally, a player could be absent for the next match for any particular reason, although there are often muscle injuries which can prevent someone from playing the next game. Knee injuries are often a common cause of complaint, while sometimes a football manager in the Premier League will rest a player due to tiredness, fatigue or just simply as a precaution after they picked up a knock. The Premier League injuries and injury list will reveal everything.

Premier League Suspensions & Bans 

In addition to Premier League injuries, it’s not uncommon for an EPL team to be missing players due to suspension. There are certain to be red cards and yellow cards issued on a frequent basis, with players being sent off leading to them being suspended for a number of games.
