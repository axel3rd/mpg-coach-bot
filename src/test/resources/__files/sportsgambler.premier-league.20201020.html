What Premier League Players are Injured?

Sometimes it’s just a handful who are unavailable, although our English Premier League injury report details how many players are missing for each EPL team. You can see the Premier League injury list at Sportsgambler.com and these inform how our predicted lineups look for each game. Sometimes there might be players rated as doubtful and it’s worth checking back to see if they’re in the confirmed lineup.

English Premier League Suspensions & Bans 

It’s not uncommon for an EPL team to be missing players due to suspension. There are certain to be red cards and yellow cards issued on a frequent basis, with players being sent off leading to them being suspended for a number of games. An accumulation of bookings will also lead to Premier League suspensions and bans, with our website profiling the players who are missing for each side so there’s no possible way they can play a part in the next game.
